Terrain Mechanics Guide

Plains: Standard chess pathing rules.
Mountains: Impassable blockades. Only Knights may vault over.
Forest: Blocks sliding views. Pieces must stop inside; Knights can jump over.
River: Strips sliding ranges. Moving pieces must stop immediately upon entry.
Ford: Shallow breaks allowing normal sliding movement directly through water.
Lake: Inner deep water blockades. Units inside cannot capture other aquatic tiles.
